15def bezierArc(x1,y1, x2,y2, startAng=0, extent=90): 

16 """bezierArc(x1,y1, x2,y2, startAng=0, extent=90) --> List of Bezier 

17curve control points. 

18 

19(x1, y1) and (x2, y2) are the corners of the enclosing rectangle. The 

20coordinate system has coordinates that increase to the right and down. 

21Angles, measured in degress, start with 0 to the right (the positive X 

22axis) and increase counter-clockwise. The arc extends from startAng 

23to startAng+extent. I.e. startAng=0 and extent=180 yields an openside-down 

24semi-circle. 

25 

26The resulting coordinates are of the form (x1,y1, x2,y2, x3,y3, x4,y4) 

27such that the curve goes from (x1, y1) to (x4, y4) with (x2, y2) and 

28(x3, y3) as their respective Bezier control points.""" 

29 

30 x1,y1, x2,y2 = min(x1,x2), max(y1,y2), max(x1,x2), min(y1,y2) 

31

34 fragAngle = float(extent) 

35 Nfrag = 1 

36 else: 

37 arcList = [] 

38 Nfrag = int(ceil(abs(extent)/90.)) 

39 fragAngle = float(extent) / Nfrag

41 x_cen = (x1+x2)/2. 

42 y_cen = (y1+y2)/2. 

43 rx = (x2-x1)/2. 

44 ry = (y2-y1)/2. 

45 halfAng = fragAngle * pi / 360. 

46 kappa = abs(4. / 3. * (1. - cos(halfAng)) / sin(halfAng))

49 sign = -1

51 sign = 1
